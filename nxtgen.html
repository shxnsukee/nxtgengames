Game publishers play a vital role in the gaming industry, as they are responsible for funding and distributing video games. This page provides an overview of game publishers, their role in the industry, and key players in the field.

Mihoyo

Cognosphere Pte., Ltd.

A Chinese video game development and publishing company. In addition to games, the company has created various products such as animated series, novels, comics, music, and merchandise.

Level Infinite

Tencent Games

Level Infinite, the western arm of global game giant Tencent, and its TiMi Studio Group, founded in 2008, are making an effort to deliver high-quality games to a global audience.

Asobimo

Asobimo Inc.

Asobimo, Inc. is a Japanese online game developer and publisher that specializes in creating visually impressive MMORPG games for smartphones.

BN Entertainment

Bandai Namco Entertainment Co.

Bandai Namco Entertainment Inc. is a Japanese multinational video game publisher headquartered in Minato-ku, Tokyo. Its international branches, Bandai Namco Entertainment America and Bandai Namco Europe, are respectively headquartered in Irvine, California, and Lyon, France.
